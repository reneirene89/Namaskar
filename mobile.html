About the Instructor

Kelly Shepard is a Healer, yoga student, freelance yoga instructor, health & wellness advocate. She has been practicing yoga & mindfulness since 2008. Former athlete & member of Federal Law Enforcement, Kelly has always lived an active lifestyle. She has found ways to integrate yoga into her life since she started practicing her freshman year at Penn State University.
